Here's a time line of Steve Jobs:

  • 31 March 1976 - Founding Apple Inc.

  • 11 July 1985 - Steve leaves Apple and started a new computer firm NeXT.
  • 11 December 1986 - Steve Jobs buys Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 17 MArch 1991 - Steve Jobs marries Laurene Powel.
  • 18 January 1993 - Toy Story work haulted and becomes NeXT Sofware Inc.
  • 19 December 1996 - Apple buys NeXT $400M.
  • 5 January 2000 - Steve Jobs is Bach as CEO at Apple Inc.
  • 22 October 2001 - Steve introduces first iPod.
  • 16 May 2005 - Steve Jobs introduces the Mac mini, iPod shuffle and Mac OS X 10.4.
  • 28 June 2007 - Steve Jobs intrudes iPhone, Apple TV and iPod Touch.
  • 13 January 2009 - Steve takes medical leave for six months.
  • 26 January 2010 - Steve introduces iPad, Macbook Air, iPhone 4 and iPod Touch 4G.
  • 23 August 2011 - Steve resigned as CEO of Apple.
  • 5 October 2011 - Jobs died at his Palo Alto, California, home around 3 p.m.
